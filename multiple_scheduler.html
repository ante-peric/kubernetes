10. Multiple Scheduler


U slučaju da defaultni scheduler ne zadovoljava naše potrebe upravljanja pod – a . Odlučujemo kreirati naš po potrebi algoritam.

Tada možemo imati na primjer:

• Kube-Scheduler
• my-scheduler1
• my-scheduler2

Kod kreiranja pod – a mi možemo narediti podu da koristi nač prilagodjenu scheduler.

Primjer:

my-scheduler1-config.yml

apiVersion: kubescheduler.config.k8s.io/v1
kind: KubeSchedulerConfiguration
profiles:
- schedulerName: my-scheduler

Kreira se pod definition.

apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
metadata:
  name: nginx
spec:
  containers:
  - image: nginx
    name: nginx-conatainer
  schedulerName: my-scheduler
